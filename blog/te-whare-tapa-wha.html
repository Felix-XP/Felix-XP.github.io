Te Whare Tapa Wha

My Well-Being Plan

I consider myself to be pretty goal orientated and mindful about my general wellbeing. I structure my routine by tasks associated to my goals, within the catagories of My Physical, Mental and Spiritual. It was a refreshing experience for me learning about Ta Mason Durie's well-being model of Te Whare Tapa Wha. I think that its special that the specific ideas and concepts are drawn from and linked too Maori symbols and concepts. Maori spirituality is special to me, being of samoan decent, where our cultures share alot of similarities and also for me as someone that claims Aotearoa as their home, I believe its appropriate to honour the Tangata Whenua, who share their land with us. Learning about Te Whare Tapa Wha and intergrating it into my Learning Plan, in some small sense allows me the opportunity to honour as such.
