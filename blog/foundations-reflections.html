Values, Empathy and Self Awareness in learning and programming

I believe that values are important in remaining true to who you are in whatever the context you find youself; whether in learning or in work environment. I believe that it through your values, you are able to create your character; the picture of yourself you want to present the world.

I believe empathy is important mostly for in regards to listening to others and journeying along side people. This can be for your learning and work spaces. I believe you should hold empathy for yourself especially in the times of stress and when programming becomes quite difficult; empathy allows you to hold space for youself where you should be kind give youself grace.

Self Awareness I believe is a powerful tool for your mental, spiritual and learning development. A tool to help recognise and manage emotions and internal processes to optimise overall performance.

The Importance of Core Skills

In dev academy core cirriculem, it was commonly mentioned that one of the most important attributes for successful employabilty, is the level of a persons core competence. I believe this to be true as well. The importance of establishing a character that is mindful and considerate of his/hers surroundings is a desirable quality to have in any workplace.

I believe the 'Core Cirriculem' has its rightful place in dev academy. I believe core skills are skills that have an endless potential for development. Being a programmer is a sub attribute of being human. It is most likely that more important role, we can adopt will manifest in our future lives, then that of being a programmer. Understanding these things you can realise core skills to be the fundemental of all skills. Improvement in this area will silmultanousley improve all other skills.
